Abstract

We establish a demonstration upper bound under a polynomial overlap-decay condition (Robins et al., 1994). Every formal statement is machine-verified in Lean 4.

Setup and assumptions

Consider the usual potential-outcomes setup with bounded outcomes. Overlap may fail in the tail at a polynomial rate.

Assumption 1 (Polynomial overlap decay).

There exist constants \(c_- , c_+, t_0\) and \(\kappa > 0\) such that \(c_- t^{\kappa} \le \Pr(U \le t) \le c_+ t^{\kappa}\) for all \(t \le t_0\).

⊢ Lean

Main results

Theorem 1 (Upper bound).

Under Assumption 1, the clipped estimator satisfies \(\mathrm{risk} \le C\, n^{-(1+\kappa)/(2+\kappa)}\).
