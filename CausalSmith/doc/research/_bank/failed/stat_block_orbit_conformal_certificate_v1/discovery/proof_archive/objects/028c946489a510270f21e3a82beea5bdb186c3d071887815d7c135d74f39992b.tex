Apply \(\texttt{prop:asymmetric-finite-rank-orbit-gap-bound}\) with \(\varepsilon_0=\varepsilon_1=\alpha/2\). \Pr_P(\tau_T\in\mathcal C_w\mid\mathcal T)

\]
The generalized elbow statement becomes \(\alpha=2j/(M+1)\), and its coordinate argument already proves \(|\delta_{a,O}(w)|\le\Delta_a(w)\).
