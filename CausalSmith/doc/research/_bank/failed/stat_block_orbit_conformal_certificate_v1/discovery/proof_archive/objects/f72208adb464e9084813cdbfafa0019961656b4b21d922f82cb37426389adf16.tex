so the diagonal elbows occur at \(\alpha=2j/(M+1)\). Finally, the coordinate conclusion of the asymmetric proposition gives \(|\delta_{a,O}(w)|\le\Delta_a(w)\) for every arm and orbit. This is exactly the claimed symmetric bound.
