Because \(F\subseteq X\), there is a disjoint decomposition\n\[X=F\mathbin{\dot\cup}(X\setminus F).\]\nFinite additivity of cardinality therefore gives\n\[|X|=|F|+|X\setminus F|\ge6+3=9>6=|F|.\]\nMoreover, \(|X\setminus F|\ge3\) implies \(X\setminus F\ne\varnothing\). Thus \(F\subseteq X\) is strict, so \(F\subsetneq X\).
