\begin{proof}[Proof of \cref{obj:lem:crossfit-localized-offset-control}]
Apply the uniform localized offset envelope of \cref{obj:ass:vc-localized-offset-envelope} with constants \(C_0,p\), and enlarge the constant to \(C=C_0K\), where \(K\) is the fixed number of folds.  The fixed-fold assumption gives \(K>0\), hence \(C>0\). % lean: crossfit_localized_offset_control

Fix \(P,n,B\) and the foldwise increments.  Let \(w_k=|I_k|/n\), let the pooled offset supremum be
\[
\sup_{\pi\in\Pi}\left\{2|G_{\mathrm{cf}}(\pi)|-\frac{R_P(\pi)}4\right\}_+,
\]
and let the \(k\)-th folded supremum be the analogous offset supremum on fold \(I_k\).  Pointwise in the sample, the pooled supremum is bounded by the weighted sum of the folded suprema, because the pooled cross-fit process is the corresponding fold-size weighted average and the positive-part offset is subadditively controlled after taking the supremum. % lean: pooledOffsetSup_pointwise_le_sum_fold

After integrating, monotonicity of the integral and the fold-projection identities reduce the pooled expectation to a sum of weighted fold expectations.  For a fold of size \(m=0\) the weight is zero.  For a nonempty fold, the localized offset envelope applies to that fold with sample size \(m\), envelope \(B\), and the same second-moment bound \(B^2P_X(D_\pi)\). % lean: integral_foldOffsetSup_eq_expected

The remaining bookkeeping compares each weighted fold rate with the target \(n\)-rate:
\[
\frac{m}{n}\left(\frac{B^2}{m}\right)^{A_\alpha}
\le
\left(\frac{B^2}{n}\right)^{A_\alpha},
\qquad
(\log m)^p\le(\log n)^p,
\]
using \(A_\alpha=(1+\alpha)/(2+\alpha)\le1\), \(m\le n\), and \(p\ge0\). % lean: fold_weight_mul_offset_rpow_le
Summing over the finitely many folds gives
\[
E_P\sup_{\pi\in\Pi}
\left\{2|G_{\mathrm{cf}}(\pi)|-\frac{R_P(\pi)}4\right\}_+
\le C\left(\frac{B^2}{n}\right)^{A_\alpha}(\log n)^p,
\]
with the enlarged constant absorbing the fold count. % lean: crossfit_localized_offset_control
\end{proof}
