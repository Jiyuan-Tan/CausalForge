# Title
**A Minimax Lower Bound for Interior Dose-Response Estimation**

**Contribution statement.** The paper proves an all-\(\beta\) minimax lower bound for estimating the interior dose-response partial mean under Hölder smoothness, and compares the certified lower rate with the published higher-order influence-function benchmark in the smooth- and low-covariate-smoothness regimes.
