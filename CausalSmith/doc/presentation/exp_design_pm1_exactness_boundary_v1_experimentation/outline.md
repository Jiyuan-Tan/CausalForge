# Title
**Exact Randomized Designs for Two-Block Interference Experiments**

**Contribution statement.** The paper characterizes when the covariance relaxation used for interference-aware randomized design is exactly attainable by a balanced $\{\pm1\}$ assignment law in a two-block homophily model, and gives a finite active-set formula for the loss when exact attainability fails.
