\begin{proof}[Proof of \cref{obj:lem:spread-vertex-certificate}]
Put \(q=2(m-1)\), \(M=2m\), and \(s=\sqrt q\).  Since \(q>0\), \(s>0\).  The point \((M/q,0,0)\) has nonnegative coordinates and satisfies \(q(M/q)+0+0=M\), so it lies in \(T_m\). % lean: spread_vertex_certificate

Let \((x,y,z_{\mathrm{sp}})\in T_m\) be different from \((M/q,0,0)\).  From \(s^2=q\) we have
\[
(sx)^2\le qx^2+y^2+z_{\mathrm{sp}}^2,
\]
hence
\[
\kappa sx\le \kappa\sqrt{qx^2+y^2+z_{\mathrm{sp}}^2}.
\]
Also
\[
\sqrt{q(M/q)^2}=s(M/q).
\]
The assumptions on \(c_y\) and \(c_z\), after multiplication by \(s>0\), give
\[
(c_y-c_x/q)s-\kappa>0,\qquad (c_z-c_x/q)s-\kappa>0.
\]
If \(y=z_{\mathrm{sp}}=0\), the simplex equation forces \(x=M/q\), contrary to the assumed inequality of points; hence \(y\) or \(z_{\mathrm{sp}}\) is positive.  Therefore
\[
0<\bigl((c_y-c_x/q)s-\kappa\bigr)y
  +\bigl((c_z-c_x/q)s-\kappa\bigr)z_{\mathrm{sp}}.
\]
Using \(M=qx+y+z_{\mathrm{sp}}\), the algebraic identity
\[
\begin{aligned}
&\Bigl(c_xx+c_yy+c_zz_{\mathrm{sp}}+\kappa sx
      -c_x(M/q)-\kappa s(M/q)\Bigr)s  \\
&\qquad =
\bigl((c_y-c_x/q)s-\kappa\bigr)y
+\bigl((c_z-c_x/q)s-\kappa\bigr)z_{\mathrm{sp}}
\end{aligned}
\]
then implies
\[
c_x(M/q)+\kappa s(M/q)
<
c_xx+c_yy+c_zz_{\mathrm{sp}}+\kappa sx .
\]
Together with the norm lower bound, this is exactly
\[
\phi(M/q,0,0)<\phi(x,y,z_{\mathrm{sp}}).
\]
% lean: spreadObjectiveDiff_mul_sqrt % lean: spread_vertex_certificate
\end{proof}
