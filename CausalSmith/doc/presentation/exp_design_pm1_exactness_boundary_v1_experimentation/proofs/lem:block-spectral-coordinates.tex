\begin{proof}[Proof of \cref{obj:lem:block-spectral-coordinates}]
\cref{obj:ass:two-block-homophily} gives \(m\ge2\), so the block-size normalizations used below are nonzero.  For \(X(u,v)\), the block-elliptope inequalities are precisely the three nonnegativity conditions
\[
1-u\ge0,\qquad 1+(m-1)u-mv\ge0,\qquad 1+(m-1)u+mv\ge0,
\]
and the coordinates satisfy the trace identity
\[
q(1-u)+\bigl(1+(m-1)u-mv\bigr)+\bigl(1+(m-1)u+mv\bigr)=2m .
\]
Thus block-elliptope membership is equivalent to \((x,y,z_{\mathrm{sp}})\in T_m\). % lean: blockSymMatrix_mem_blockElliptope_iff_reducedTriangle

For the objective formula, compute the four terms on \(X(u,v)\).  Pair counting gives
\[
\operatorname{tr}(J_nX(u,v))=2m\,z_{\mathrm{sp}},
\qquad
\|X(u,v)\|_{S_2}
=\sqrt{qx^2+y^2+z_{\mathrm{sp}}^2}.
\]
The projections onto the all-ones and block-sign directions give traces \(z_{\mathrm{sp}}\) and \(y\), respectively, and the diagonal trace is \(2m\).  From the two-block Laplacian and its spectral-action pseudoinverse, this yields
\[
\operatorname{tr}(L_mX(u,v))=q(a+b)x+2b\,y,
\qquad
\operatorname{tr}(L_m^\dagger X(u,v))=\frac{q x}{a+b}+\frac{y}{2b}.
\]
Substituting these four identities into \cref{obj:def:design-objective} gives
\[
F_{r,\kappa}(X(u,v))
=c_xx+c_yy+c_zz_{\mathrm{sp}}
+\kappa\sqrt{qx^2+y^2+z_{\mathrm{sp}}^2}.
\]
% lean: block_spectral_coordinates

Finally, the cut covariance is the block matrix with within-block entries \(1\) and across-block entries \(-1\), hence \(X_{\mathrm{cut}}=X(1,-1)\), and direct substitution gives \((0,2m,0)\).  Likewise the identity matrix is \(X(0,0)\), and direct substitution gives \((1,1,1)\). % lean: cutCovariance_eq_blockSym % lean: identity_eq_blockSym
\end{proof}
